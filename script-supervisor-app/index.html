Script Supervisor App for Continuity and Daily Reports

ClapKit is a script supervisor app designed for fast continuity-focused logging on active film sets.

The problem on set

Script supervisors often split notes between paper sheets, stopwatch apps, and spreadsheets after wrap. That creates duplicated work and increases continuity risk.

How ClapKit helps

  • Track scenes, shots, takes, and statuses in one workflow
  • Capture notes and timing directly per take
  • Keep camera parameters attached to the same entry
  • Export clean CSV/XLSX reports at wrap

Why teams adopt it

Instead of rewriting reports late at night, teams capture production truth once and share structured output immediately.
