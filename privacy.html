Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 17, 2026

1. Data We Collect

ClapKit does not require registration and does not collect personal data through in-app forms. We do not use tracking, ad identifiers, or third-party behavioral analytics.

2. Where Data Is Stored

Data you create in the app (projects, shooting days, scenes, shots, takes, notes, and technical parameters) is stored locally on your device.

3. Export and Data Transfer

Export to .clap, CSV, and XLSX happens only when you initiate it. Files are created on your device and shared only if you choose to share them through iOS system tools.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may open external links, including email support. When you use third-party services, their own privacy policies and terms apply.

5. Children

ClapKit is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as app features or legal requirements change. The current version is published with a new effective date.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: clapkit.pro@gmail.com
